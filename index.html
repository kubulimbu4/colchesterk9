About us

At Colchester K9 Rescue, we prioritize the welfare of every dog we rescue from various situations. Our dedicated team, composed of experienced dog professionals, ensures that each dog receives lifelong care and support. We work closely with a trusted veterinary team to address all medical needs, including spaying/neutering, to prepare them for their forever homes. Prospective adopters must complete an online application form, followed by a thorough home check to ensure suitability. We carefully listen to each applicant's preferences to match them with the most suitable dog. A minimum donation is required for all adoptions to sustain our rescue efforts.
